# Strategy — Adjacent-Niche Backlink Partnerships

## The thesis
Blue Logic ranks #1 for its brand and almost nothing commercial. The whole local
water-treatment niche is low-authority (Authority Score 5–16), so a *handful* of real,
relevant links pulls us ahead of competitors. The existing backlink profile is ~95% spam
(disavow file on hand). The growth lever is **earned, relevant links** — built by giving
adjacent businesses something genuinely useful to link to.

## Core principle: build the magnet, then trade
A backlink *exchange* needs two assets:
- **Link-IN target (the magnet):** a utility/data page so useful that a partner links to
  it *for their own audience's benefit* — no sales friction, editor-approvable.
- **Link-OUT currency:** a way to reciprocate (feature them, guest post, list them on a
  "Trusted Utah Home Pros" resources page).

We never buy links, never join reciprocal link farms, never use private blog networks.
Every link must pass: **relevance + real organic traffic + real business.**

## The 4 partner niches (non-competing, same audience = Utah homeowners)
1. **Appliance sales & repair / water-heater installers** — hard water is *the* villain
   killing water heaters & dishwashers. Most natural content marriage, easiest yes.
2. **Custom home builders & remodelers** — new construction = our install window; many run
   "preferred trade partner" pages (higher DA).
3. **Well drilling & well/septic services** — rural Utah on wells *needs* treatment;
   strictly non-competing; they refer treatment out.
4. **Real estate agents & home inspectors** — relocation & home-buying guides love a
   "test your water" resource; inspectors who run water tests are aligned.

Honorable mentions for later volume: HVAC, kitchen-&-bath remodelers (under-sink RO),
pool & spa, local coffee roasters (PR angle).

## Per-partnership workflow
1. **Identify** prospects (Google + Semrush) for the target niche.
2. **Audit quality** in Semrush — Authority Score, stable organic traffic (not a spam
   spike), has a blog or resources/partners page, is a real business.
3. **Pitch** value-first (templates in `outreach-templates.md`).
4. **Deliver** value — guest post / expert quote feature / resource listing / data citation.
5. **Secure the link** to the *relevant* asset.
6. **Track & monitor** — Semrush `backlinks_*`, GSC links report.

## Quality bar / guardrails
- No paid links, no link schemes, no reciprocal link farming (Google Spam Policies).
- Relevance + real traffic required before outreach.
- Keep it value-add for *both* audiences — if a normal reader wouldn't benefit, skip it.
- Protect the brand: monitor for new spam links; disavow only on manual action / growing spam.

## KPIs (review monthly)
- Referring domains from **Authority Score ≥ 20**, topically-relevant Utah sites.
- Organic sessions to the 5 asset pages.
- Rankings for each asset's target keywords (see `content-assets.md`).
- Net new keywords ranking in positions 1–20.
