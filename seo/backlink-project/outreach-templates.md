# Outreach Templates

> **Style rules (Scott's preference, 2026-06-17):** NO em dashes (use periods/commas);
> clean direct URLs only (no redirect/tracking/shortened links, they feel spammy). Plain,
> human, value-first. **Bake the signature into the body** — Gmail's auto-signature does NOT
> apply to API-created drafts. Use exactly:
> ```
> Scott Watson, Owner
> P: (801) 980-2583
> https://www.bluelogicwater.com
> ```
>
> Fill the **sender identity block** in `access-and-setup.md` first; these templates pull
> from it. Keep every email short, specific, and value-first. One ask per email.
> Personalize the bracketed bits per prospect; never send the raw template.

## Subject lines (A/B)
- "Quick collaboration idea for [Their Business]'s readers"
- "A free Utah water resource for your [home buyers / clients]"
- "Featuring [Their Business] in a Utah homeowner guide"

## Niche-customized angles
**Appliance & water-heater pros (asset #3/#1):** lead with "hard water is the #1 reason
water heaters fail early — I built a guide + a city hardness lookup your customers can use."

**Home builders & remodelers (asset #5/#1):** lead with "I put together a transparent
Utah water-system cost guide your new-build clients can reference when budgeting."

**Well & septic (asset #4/#1):** lead with "you drill/service wells but don't do
treatment — here's a rural-Utah well water treatment guide you can hand customers who ask."

**Realtors & inspectors (asset #2/#1):** lead with "I made a 'Moving to Utah' new-homeowner
water checklist your buyers would find handy — happy to co-brand or guest write."

## Follow-up sequence (no reply)
- **+4 business days:** one-line bump — "Floating this back up — worth a quick chat?"
- **+10 business days:** final, give-an-out — "No worries if it's not a fit; I'll leave the
  offer open if you ever want a Utah water resource for your readers."
- **Stop after 3 total touches.** Log outcome in `prospects.md`.

## Rules
- Never send identical copy to multiple prospects in the same batch (spam signal).
- No "link exchange" language in the email — frame as audience value / collaboration.
- Track every send + reply in `prospects.md`.
