## What is Reverse Osmosis?

Reverse osmosis is a water purification technology that uses a semi-permeable membrane to remove ions, unwanted molecules, and other impurities from water.

### How It Works

The RO process works by applying pressure to force water molecules through a membrane, leaving contaminants behind. This results in:

- **Up to 99% reduction** of dissolved solids
- **Superior taste and purity**
- **Healthier water** without the contaminants

Utah's water naturally contains high levels of minerals (hard water). Our RO systems are specifically designed for the Wasatch Front's water chemistry, ensuring optimal performance.
