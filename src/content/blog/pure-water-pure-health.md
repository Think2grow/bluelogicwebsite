## What Is Reverse Osmosis?

Reverse osmosis (RO) is a water purification process that uses pressure to force water through a semi-permeable membrane with pores so small — 0.0001 micron — that only water molecules pass through. Dissolved contaminants, minerals, and other impurities are left behind and flushed to drain.

### How It Works

Water is pushed through the RO membrane under pressure. The membrane allows water molecules through while blocking:

- **Dissolved minerals** — calcium, magnesium, and other hardness compounds
- **Heavy metals** — including arsenic, lead, and chromium-6
- **Dissolved organic contaminants** — including PFAS compounds
- **Disinfection byproducts** — haloacetic acids and trihalomethanes

The result is water that has had the vast majority of its dissolved content removed — purer, better-tasting, and free from the contaminants that standard filtration and softening leave behind.

### Whole-Home RO vs. Point-of-Use RO

Most people are familiar with under-sink RO filters that treat a single kitchen faucet. [Whole-home reverse osmosis systems](/whole-home-reverse-osmosis-utah/) take the same technology and apply it to every tap, shower, and appliance in the home — using a pressurized storage tank and a delivery pump to maintain full household flow and pressure.

## Why Utah Homes Benefit From RO

Utah's Wasatch Front has some of the hardest water in the United States, and multiple communities have water supplies flagged by the Environmental Working Group for contaminants including arsenic, chromium-6, PFAS, and haloacetic acids — all at levels above EWG health guidelines while remaining federally compliant.

A [standard water softener](/water-softener-utah/) addresses hardness only. A [whole-home filtration system](/whole-home-water-filtration/) addresses hardness, chlorine, iron, and manganese. Only reverse osmosis reaches the molecular level to address arsenic, heavy metals, and PFAS. For Utah communities with elevated contaminant profiles — particularly [Lehi](/locations/lehi/), [Draper](/locations/draper/), [West Jordan](/locations/west-jordan/), and [Salt Lake City](/locations/salt-lake-city/) — whole-home RO is the most complete residential solution available.
